Welcome!

My name is Dylan Liesenfelt, and I’m a Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence student at Florida Atlantic University. My interest in computers began at a young age, when my stepbrother showed me how to mod Halo on our Xbox.

After graduating high school, I joined the U.S. Navy in 2014 as an Aviation Electrician’s Mate. In 2022, I left the service with an Honorable Discharge and returned home to further my education. Drawing on my troubleshooting skills from the Navy and my lifelong passion for technology, I decided to major in Computer Science.

I received my Associate’s degree with honors from Broward College in 2024. Soon after, I began working on my Bachelor’s degree at Florida Atlantic University, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Artificial Intelligence. I’m on track to graduate in Spring 2026.

Currently, I live in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, with my girlfriend and our three Siberian Huskies. Thank you for stopping by—please feel free to look around!
